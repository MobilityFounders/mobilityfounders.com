CARSHARING YOUR WAY

CarMoov enables car rental companies to offer Carsharing services to their customers thanks to a proprietary platform.

 

CarMoov makes it possible for you to operate from virtually anywhere, be it railway stations, hotels, harbours, or your doorstep… We believe customers should be able to book, pickup or get cars delivered 24/7, with a secured, fast and simple mobile process!

Our Carsharing system is built to function in coordination with you existing software. It is very easy to tailor, implement and use.

« ON DEMAND » AGENCIES !

Our solution enables car rental agencies to create, display and manage « Access points ». You define a pick up and drop off areas thanks to our geofencing tools and create dozens of « New 24/7 agencies ».

Make your cars available in specific locations such as Railway stations, Hotels, Convention centers,… without having to open a brick and mortar agency.

SYNCED WITH YOUR EXISTING PLATFORM

CarMoov is integrated with your existing software. Your fleet is connected with our technology and you rent your cars either in a classic way, or on a 24/7 keyless mode. Our interfaces handle both activities.

Our call center can be integrated in your interface and assist your customers outside opening hours.

A MOBILE WEBSITE SERVING YOUR CUSTOMERS

Our responsive mobile website enables your customers to book, fill personnal information, pay the deposit, do the check in, open the car and pay the rent directly from their mobile phone without having to download an app or sign up for a plan!

You can access and manage the customer information and check-in/check out documents directly from your computer.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

1

Our engineers connect CarMoov and your existing software.

2

We tailor the customer facing and user facing interfaces according to your needs.

3

Your existing software and CarMoov are connected via secured APIs, you can now offer your services 24/7 and manage CarSharing using CarMoov!
